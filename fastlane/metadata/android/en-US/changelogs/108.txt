New:
- Refreshed Find screen with a more polished glassy result layout and the `findsong.png` action button restored
- Added optional miniplayer artwork progress outline under General Settings > Now Playing Indicator

Improved:
- Find suggestions now show live download progress instead of a static download button while a song is being saved
- Crossfade metadata handoff is smoother across the player UI, mini-player, and notification surfaces
- Crossfade fallback now keeps the current song stable if the incoming source fails during the transition
- Lyric share preview is more compact, with tap-to-start line selection and quick 1/3/6/9/11 line count chips
- Queue Discover now runs immediately from the floating banner and reports when it finishes

Fixed:
- Better distinction between no-internet playback failures and source/extractor failures
- Source drift errors such as parsing/extractor issues now retry more safely instead of being treated like random connection loss
- Reduced abrupt playback exits caused by crossfade secondary-player failures
- Miniplayer artwork progress now follows the selected thumbnail roundness instead of drawing sharp corners
- Rewind top song, artist, and album slides now show up to 10 entries instead of stopping at 5
